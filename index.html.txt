  <section id="systems" class="systems">
    <h2 class="section-title">Systems & Leadership Proof</h2>
    
    <div class="flagship-card">
      <h2>BiodiverseCities Poster System</h2>
      <p class="meta">IUCN Europe | November 2025 | Systems Design Leadership</p>
      
      <div class="challenge">
        <h3>The Challenge</h3>
        <p>Create consistent, high-quality posters for 12+ EU pilot cities without requiring a designer for each one. Every pilot city has different context, but the same quality bar. How do you scale design without scaling the designer?</p>
      </div>

      <div class="solution">
        <h3>The Solution: A 5-Step Documented System</h3>
        <p>I built a workflow that combines Canva template (locked structure, variable content), Excel content collection (validation rules), QC checklist, EU compliance framework, and step-by-step instructions anyone can execute.</p>
        
        <div class="step">
          <h4>Step 1: Content Collection</h4>
          <p class="step-owner">Owner: Project Coordinator | Time: 30 minutes</p>
          <p>Send Excel template to pilot lead. They fill in: city name, context, challenges, goals, actions, key lessons. Validation rules flag incomplete fields. Progress tracker shows completion percentage.</p>
        </div>

        <div class="step">
          <h4>Step 2: Content Review & Refinement</h4>
          <p class="step-owner">Owner: Communications Team | Time: 20 minutes</p>
          <p><strong>Quality checks:</strong> Key lesson is specific (not vague). Challenges include data/numbers. Context is LOCAL, not generic. Actions are concrete, not hopes. No jargon citizens wouldn't understand.</p>
          <p><strong>Example fix:</strong> "We improved biodiversity" ‚Üí "Native pollinator species increased 34% in 18 months"</p>
        </div>

        <div class="step">
          <h4>Step 3: Design Production</h4>
          <p class="step-owner">Owner: Communications Team | Time: 90 min (first) ‚Üí 45 min (subsequent)</p>
          <p>Duplicate template. Copy/paste content from Excel. Replace placeholder image. Update city/country. Check EU compliance. Design is LOCKED (no fonts, colors, layout changes). This prevents scope creep and ensures consistency.</p>
        </div>

        <div class="step">
          <h4>Step 4: Compliance & Quality Check</h4>
          <p class="step-owner">Owner: Senior Communications Officer | Time: 10 minutes</p>
          <p><strong>EU Compliance:</strong> EU flag is larger than any institutional logo. Interreg logo present. "Co-funded by the European Union" visible. All partner logos same size or smaller than EU flag.</p>
          <p><strong>Content Quality:</strong> No typos. Facts verified. Key lesson prominent. Image print-ready. City/country correct.</p>
        </div>

        <div class="step">
          <h4>Step 5: Approval & Distribution</h4>
          <p class="step-owner">Owner: Project Manager | Time: 5 minutes</p>
          <p>Send PDF proof to pilot lead (48-hour sign-off). Make corrections. Export files (PDF 300dpi CMYK for print, PDF 150dpi RGB for digital, PNG 1080x1080 for social). Archive in SharePoint with naming convention.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="impact">
        <h3>Impact</h3>
        <p><strong>Time savings:</strong> 8 hours ‚Üí 2 hours per poster (75% reduction)</p>
        <p><strong>Scalability:</strong> Works for all 12+ BiodiverseCities pilots without designer bottleneck</p>
        <p><strong>Compliance:</strong> EU rules built into process, not dependent on people remembering</p>
        <p><strong>Sustainability:</strong> System documented so it survives team changes (actually improves the org)</p>
        <p><strong>Quality:</strong> Consistent across all cities, automated checks, built-in standards</p>
        <p style="margin-top: 1.5rem;"><strong>Why this matters:</strong> This proves you don't optimize for heroics‚Äîyou optimize for systems. Good systems reduce dependency on one person, embed quality in the process itself, document so knowledge transfers, and scale without proportional cost increase.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>
