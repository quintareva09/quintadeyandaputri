Systems & Leadership Proof BiodiverseCities Poster System IUCN Europe | November 2025 | Systems Design Leadership The Challenge Create consistent, high-quality posters for 12+ EU pilot cities without requiring a designer for each one. Every pilot city has different context, but the same quality bar. How do you scale design without scaling the designer? The Solution: A 5-Step Documented System I built a workflow that combines Canva template (locked structure, variable content), Excel content collection (validation rules), QC checklist, EU compliance framework, and step-by-step instructions anyone can execute. Step 1: Content Collection Owner: Project Coordinator | Time: 30 minutes Send Excel template to pilot lead. They fill in: city name, context, challenges, goals, actions, key lessons. Validation rules flag incomplete fields. Progress tracker shows completion percentage. Step 2: Content Review & Refinement Owner: Communications Team | Time: 20 minutes Quality checks: Key lesson is specific (not vague). Challenges include data/numbers. Context is LOCAL, not generic. Actions are concrete, not hopes. No jargon citizens wouldn't understand. Example fix: "We improved biodiversity" ‚Üí "Native pollinator species increased 34% in 18 months" Step 3: Design Production Owner: Communications Team | Time: 90 min (first) ‚Üí 45 min (subsequent) Duplicate template. Copy/paste content from Excel. Replace placeholder image. Update city/country. Check EU compliance. Design is LOCKED (no fonts, colors, layout changes). This prevents scope creep and ensures consistency. Step 4: Compliance & Quality Check Owner: Senior Communications Officer | Time: 10 minutes EU Compliance: EU flag is larger than any institutional logo. Interreg logo present. "Co-funded by the European Union" visible. All partner logos same size or smaller than EU flag. Content Quality: No typos. Facts verified. Key lesson prominent. Image print-ready. City/country correct. Step 5: Approval & Distribution Owner: Project Manager | Time: 5 minutes Send PDF proof to pilot lead (48-hour sign-off). Make corrections. Export files (PDF 300dpi CMYK for print, PDF 150dpi RGB for digital, PNG 1080x1080 for social). Archive in SharePoint with naming convention. Impact Time savings: 8 hours ‚Üí 2 hours per poster (75% reduction) Scalability: Works for all 12+ BiodiverseCities pilots without designer bottleneck Compliance: EU rules built into process, not dependent on people remembering Sustainability: System documented so it survives team changes (actually improves the org) Quality: Consistent across all cities, automated checks, built-in standards Why this matters: This proves you don't optimize for heroics‚Äîyou optimize for systems. Good systems reduce dependency on one person, embed quality in the process itself, document so knowledge transfers, and scale without proportional cost increase.
